How it works

A three-sided platform with a white-labeled distribution layer.

Safasoft aggregates DMC supply on one side, distributes it through wholesaler-branded portals, and surfaces it to downstream retail agencies. Each side gets a purpose-built portal; the platform handles curation, contracts, payments, translation, and quality control.

07 / 12
Platform capabilities

What the system includes today.

Beyond the four portals, the platform handles the operational primitives that make the product work end-to-end.

Multi-tenant theming

Each wholesaler gets a subdomain, brand colors, logo, voucher header. CSS-variable based — adding tenants is config, not code.

Bilingual-native

Every UI string keyed. Every itinerary, day, agency, city stored bilingually. Per-portal locale defaults; one-click manual override.

Layered margin model

DMC net → platform markup → wholesaler markup → agency retail. Every layer visible to the parties that own it. Live edit.

Dual-mode wallet

Prepaid (top-up + draw down) or credit (line of credit + monthly settlement). Configurable per-agency. Full transaction ledger.

SLA & RFQ tracking

24h response windows with live countdown. Color-coded urgency. Escalation path to platform when wholesaler can't respond.

Curation & publishing

Per-wholesaler supply filtering. Per-agency publish toggles. Draft → review → publish workflow with translation checkpoint.

Capacity management

Per-departure allotments (FIXED) or weekly rolling capacity (ON_DEMAND). Automatic guaranteed/open/full status based on fill rate.

Co-branded vouchers

White voucher document with wholesaler header + DMC footer. Booking ref, pax data, day-by-day services, supplier info.

Wholesaler analytics

GMV by month, top agencies, top itineraries, conversion funnel. Real-time aggregates. Cross-tenant view for platform admins.

Business model

Transaction-based revenue, aligned with wholesaler success.

The platform takes a transparent markup layer on every booking. No subscription required to get started; no per-seat licensing; no setup fees. We earn when our partners earn.

Per-booking platform margin

A fixed markup per pax on every booking, tiered by itinerary type. Contractually locked with each wholesaler — they see the number on day one and it doesn't change.
